 Mahendra Singh Dhoni ( born 7 July 1981) is an Indian professional cricketer who plays as a right handed batter and a wicket-keeper 



 
Allu Arjun
 

 Allu Arjun (born 8 April 1982) is an Indian actor who works in Telugu cinema. One of the highest-paid actors in Indian cinema ,Allu Arjun has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list since 2014.
